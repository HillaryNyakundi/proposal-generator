## Overview

AutoHelpX is a roadside-assistance platform for Zimbabwe. It connects stranded
drivers with nearby **mechanics**, **tow-truck operators**, and **spare-parts
sellers**, handling the whole journey from a breakdown to a completed, rated,
and paid-for job, all in real time.

This document summarizes the features currently built and live on the platform.

**At a glance**

| | |
|---|---|
| **4 kinds of help** | Mechanic · Tow truck · Jump start · Fuel delivery |
| **4 member roles** | Driver · Mechanic · Tow-truck operator · Parts seller (plus Admin) |
| **Live tracking** | Drivers watch help approach on a map in real time |
| **Mobile money** | Top-ups and payments through EcoCash |
| **1-month free** | Every new member starts with a free period - no fees |

## 1. Accounts & getting started

- **Sign up in one of four roles** - driver, mechanic, tow-truck operator, or
  parts seller. The role shapes what a member sees and can do.
- **Log in with email _or_ phone number** - whichever the member prefers.
- **A welcoming start** - every new member gets a **1-month free period** with no
  service fees or commissions, and a welcome email that explains it.
- **Forgotten password, handled safely** - members receive a **6-digit code by
  email** (valid for 15 minutes) to reset their password. No risky reset links.
- **Manage your profile** - update details and add a profile photo at any time.
- **Delete your account yourself** - a full self-service option (see §9).

## 2. The rescue journey - tracked end to end

A driver always knows exactly where their help is. Every request moves through
clear stages that both the driver and the provider can follow live:

**Requested → Accepted → On the way → Arrived → In progress → Completed**

| Stage | What it means |
|---|---|
| **Requested** | The driver has posted the request; nearby providers are alerted. |
| **Accepted** | A provider takes the job. The service fee applies to the driver (waived during their free period). |
| **On the way** | The provider is travelling to the breakdown. |
| **Arrived** | The provider is on-site. |
| **In progress** | Work is underway. |
| **Completed** | Job done. The service fee applies to the provider (waived during their free period). |

The driver can **cancel while a job is still pending or accepted** - the request
is removed and any fee already charged is **refunded automatically**.

## 3. For drivers

- **Four kinds of help** - call a mechanic, a tow truck, a jump start, or fuel
  delivery, whichever the situation needs.
- **Pin the breakdown** - share exact location plus car details (make, model,
  year, plate) so the right provider arrives prepared.
- **Fuel delivery, specified** - choose petrol or diesel and the number of litres.
- **Watch help approach** - a live map shows the provider moving toward you.
- **Change your mind** - cancel before work begins; automatic, instant refund if
  you'd already been charged.
- **Rate your rescue** - leave a star rating and review after every completed job.

## 4. For service providers (mechanics & tow trucks)

- **Jobs matched to you** - a live feed of nearby open requests that fit your
  service type. Mechanics see mechanic, jump-start, and fuel-delivery jobs;
  tow-truck operators see towing jobs.
- **Accept and go** - take a job in one tap; your status is set to *busy*
  automatically so you're not double-booked.
- **Keep the driver posted** - advance the job through each stage and stream your
  location so the customer always knows where you are.
- **Step back if you must** - if you can't finish a job, release it back to the
  queue. The driver is **refunded and re-matched** with no penalty to them, and
  you're freed up for other work.
- **Clear when a driver cancels** - if a driver cancels a job you'd already
  accepted, it moves to a **"cancelled by car owner"** stage and stays in your job
  history, so nothing simply disappears mid-task.
- **A profile that wins work** - an approval badge, star rating, and
  jobs-completed count on your public profile build trust.

## 5. Fairness & reliability - no-shows and timeouts

AutoHelpX protects drivers from providers who accept a job and then go quiet.
**If a provider stops responding, the platform steps in automatically** - no
driver is left stranded waiting.

Every few minutes the system checks for jobs where the provider has gone dark.
Each stage has a **grace period**, and later stages are more generous because the
provider is genuinely working:

| If a job is stuck at… | …for longer than | What happens |
|---|---|---|
| **Accepted** (never got moving) | 15 minutes | |
| **On the way** | 45 minutes | The job is returned to the queue, |
| **Arrived** (not started) | 30 minutes | the driver is **refunded**, both sides |
| **In progress** | 2 hours | are notified, and a **no-show** is recorded. |

*(These windows are current settings and can be tuned.)*

**Repeat no-shows are penalised.** When a provider reaches **3 no-shows**, their
account is **automatically suspended** - they can't accept new jobs until an
administrator reviews and reinstates them. Admins are alerted both in the app and
by email when this happens.

The result: drivers get reliable service, and good providers aren't undercut by
unreliable ones.

## 6. Parts marketplace

- **Rich listings** - sellers post parts with photos, price, stock levels, and
  condition (new, used, or refurbished).
- **Browse & search** - buyers explore available parts and find what fits.
- **Simple ordering** - place an order from a seller and pay securely; stock
  updates automatically as parts sell.
- **Fulfilment & cancellation** - sellers mark orders delivered; either side can
  cancel before completion, and stock is restored.

## 7. Payments, wallet & fees

Everything runs through an in-app **wallet** topped up with **EcoCash** mobile
money - the way people already pay.

- **Top up with EcoCash** - load the wallet in seconds.
- **A generous free start** - the first **month is free** for every member: no
  service fees for drivers or providers, and no seller commission.
- **Simple, predictable fees** (after the free period):
  - A **flat service fee, 50 ZWL** applies once per job - to the **driver** when a provider
    accepts, and to the **provider** when the job is completed.
  - Parts sellers pay a **5% commission** on each sale, taken only when they
    fulfil the order.
- **No surprises at request time** - a driver can only post a request they can
  afford to see through; otherwise they're prompted to top up first.
- **Refunds that just happen** - cancel an eligible request, or have a provider
  step back, and the money returns to the wallet automatically.
- **Full transaction history** - every top-up, fee, commission, and refund is
  recorded and viewable at any time.
- **A heads-up before fees begin** - members get a reminder email about **3 days
  before** their free period ends, so a top-up is never a surprise.

## 8. Staying informed - real-time & notifications

- **Live location streaming** - provider movement updates continuously on the
  driver's map during an active job.
- **Instant notifications** - alerts for new requests, acceptances, a provider on
  the way, arrivals, completed jobs, and wallet activity.
- **Synced across devices** - read a notification on one device and it's marked
  read everywhere, so there's no duplicate noise.
- **Built to scale** - the real-time system is designed to stay fast and reliable
  as the number of active users grows.

## 9. Account & privacy control

Members are in full control of their own account.

- **Delete your account yourself** - immediate and self-service, with **no waiting
  on an admin**. To prevent accidental deletion, the member confirms by typing
  their own email, and can add an optional reason.
- **Protected against loss** - deletion is blocked if the member has a job or
  order still in progress, so nothing is left half-finished.
- **Personal data is wiped** - name, email, phone, photo, password, saved
  location and movement history, and personal notifications are all removed, and
  the member is signed out everywhere.
- **Records stay honest** - anonymised transaction and job history is kept for the
  other party's records and the financial audit trail, but with no personal
  details attached.
- **A friendly goodbye** - a farewell email is sent to the member's real address,
  and they're welcome to sign up again with the same email any time.

## 10. Operations & oversight (for administrators)

Administrators keep the platform healthy, safe, and growing from one place.

- **At-a-glance dashboard** - live counts of pending approvals, total members,
  open requests, and revenue.
- **Approve & reject providers** - review each provider's details and approve or
  reject them before they can take work. Both outcomes notify the provider (a
  rejection can include a reason).
- **Manage members** - search, filter, adjust roles, and remove accounts.
- **Monitor activity** - read-only oversight of every service request and wallet
  transaction across the platform.
- **Kept in the loop by email** - admins are emailed on every new sign-up, when a
  member deletes their own account, and when a provider is auto-suspended for
  repeated no-shows.

## 11. Trust & safety

- **Vetted providers only** - every mechanic, tow operator, and seller is reviewed
  and approved by an admin before they can take work.
- **Reputation earned in the open** - drivers rate each completed job, so quality
  providers stand out.
- **Approval badges** - public profiles clearly show verified, approved status.
- **Secure sign-in** - email or phone login, safe code-based password reset, and
  self-service account control.

## 12. Every email AutoHelpX sends

All emails are branded and sent reliably; a delivery hiccup never blocks the
action behind it.

**To members:**

| Email | When it's sent |
|---|---|
| **Welcome** | On sign-up - introduces the 1-month free period. |
| **Password reset code** | When a reset is requested - a 6-digit code that expires in 15 minutes. |
| **Free-period ending** | About 3 days before the free month ends - a nudge to top up. |
| **Provider approved** | When a provider's profile is approved - they can now go live. |
| **Provider not approved** | When a profile is rejected - includes an optional reason and how to resubmit. |
| **Goodbye** | After account deletion - sent to the member's original email address. |

**To administrators:**

| Email | When it's sent |
|---|---|
| **New sign-up** | Every time someone registers - includes their email and role. |
| **Account deleted** | When a member deletes their own account - a record, with the optional reason. |
| **Provider auto-suspended** | When a provider hits the no-show limit - prompting review. |

## In summary

*One platform, from breakdown to back on the road: requesting help, matching a
provider, tracking them live, paying by mobile money, buying the parts, and rating
the service. AutoHelpX handles the whole journey.*
